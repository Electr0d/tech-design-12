2.2 - Design Criteria
General
  • Must include at least 7 components
  • Needs to explain well the consequences of catching the Coronavirus
  • Must not appear floating, perfect, or custom-made.
Video
  • Video must be in 1080p
  • Video must be in 60fps
  • Video must include sound effects
  • Video must be of a cinematic aspect-ratio
Animation
  • Should be set to high contrast if video is looking washed out
  • All objects must be textured
  • Animation must be in 60fps
  • Must be in a well-lit environment
  • Lighting must be dynamic (changing, instead of constant) to make the viewer entertained
  • Objects must not clip into each other
Audio
  • Must include sound effects for every component
  • Must have ambience noise
  • Audio should include references
Components
  • Should convey a message, directly, or indirectly
  • Get infected
  • Forced to isolate
  • Lose job
  • Get evicted
  • Quality of life drops
  • Spread the virus to others
  • Death is likely if old and have a chronic condition
  • The patient isn't in optimal health after recovery
