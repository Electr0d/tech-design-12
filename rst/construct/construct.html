Scoping out the Assignment

I have to work on this assignment during class time, which means I donâ€™t have a lot of time to design a big room. I realized this when I was looking at designs of the living room. So many components will simply take too long to model, such as the couches, chairs, and the counter. So, I decided to model my room instead since there is only the chair that I have to model. Unfortunately I cannot go into detail on how I designed my room since I am limited on time.

Planning

I began researching what I can do with my room, what I can include and what not. I made a specific criteria that the design has to follow. Then I started researching what modern room designs, furniture looks like and what I can do to make it my own design. Lights, desks, wood, etc.

Sketching

I sketched 2 designs of my room. Since my room is very small, there weren't a lot of options to choose from. For example, I canâ€™t move my bed around because I want to keep it close to the window and outlet. The only options I have are the dresser and the bookcase, so I wanted to completely redesign the dresser since the one I have currently is small and is really ugly. So I sketched out 2 room layouts and began working on the 3D design.

Modelling

Modelling was the most challenging part, I have to model everything in a short period of time before I even get started with the layout of the room. I started taking measurements around my room and putting them into Blender. Then I modelled the bed, chair, desk, drawers. Since I was very low on time, I only modelled what you get to see. For example, you never get to open the drawers, so I didnâ€™t model the track and the actual drawer, just the door. I often remodelled a lot of stuff that I already modelled 2 years ago because the mesh was really bad.

Layout

After I finished modelling most of the room, I started trying different layouts and seeing how my room looks. Design #2 was proven to work better since it makes better use of vertical space. Therefore, I sticked with design #2.

Shading

I added materials to each and every model. I mostly stick with procedural textures since they are good enough, because going online and looking for textures would take up too much time especially since most textures have a watermark on them and you have to pay to get high quality textures. I borrowed some textures from my older projects and made modifications and improvements.

Evaluation

After finishing everything, I asked for some opinions on what I should change, and made these changes. One was the material of the dresser, It was white wood. My mom suggested I change the material to a brown wood, which turned out to be a lot better. I also extended the desk because it feels cramped; and finally, I Changed the material of the bedroom door, which was a white door, like the closet doors, to a brown door.
