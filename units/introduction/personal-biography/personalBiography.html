2D Design
I am not as interested in 2D models as 3D, but I still work on designing logos, pixel art, etc. from time to time. The reason is that it is not as challenging as 3D design. For example, with 2D design, you can just put boxes on top of each other and you got a design, but with 3D design, it's not that easy. The 3D model has to have good topology to be used for rigging, shading (texturing), and animation. This is a recurring problem that beginners have to overcome. I've had my fair share of models with horrible topology when I first started.
