2.4 - Ideation
Design

This design process is going to be recorded and included in the final assignment as a time-lapse.

Since this design process is component-based, I am creating 2 designs per-component.

I am aiming for a more elaborate animation than last time, such as stacking books on top of each other instead of using ramps that look too good.

The books covers are easter eggs intended to convey a message or raise awareness about current crises, including the extermination of Uyghur Muslims by China, the racial profiling of Middle-Easters, etc.

Component 1: getting infected

This is where it all begins, when the dark ball gets in proximity with the grey ball, it turns black, since it is infected.

This design is made of simple books stacked on top of each other, realistic, but looks cheap. The books covers are easter eggs , they convey a message or raise awareness about current crises, including the extermination of Uyghur Muslims by China.

Component 3: lose job

The self-isolating makes the person lose their job since they stop going. I could represent this with:

Design #1
When the box is dropped, it cuts off a string that had been attached to the ball, the string is attached to a bucket on the other end with a pulley in between, the bucket drops to the ground, turning off the lights in the process and making a mess.
Design #2
Another design I have in mind is for the box to hit the light switch and turn off the lights as it's going down.
Component 4: get evicted

The light switching off represents no longer being able to maintain the house, the eviction component can be represented with two designs:

Design #1
The container box is lifted on one end and the ball is pushed outside.
Design #2
A door on the container box opens and the ball is pushed outside.
Component 5: quality of life drops

Since the person is now homeless, sick, and jobless, their quality of life is significantly diminished. This can be illustrated with:

Design #1
The ball triggers a weight being put on a bucket connected with another bucket by a string and a pulley in between. the quality of life bucket drops down as the new weight overwhelms the other bucket.
Design #2
The ball triggers a trapdoor at the bottom of a bucket, opening it. The bucket loses all its contents, decreasing the weight reading.
Component 6: spread the virus to others

The person spreads the virus to others surrounding them. This can be shown with:

Design #1
The ball goes between other spheres, infecting them.
Design #2
The ball stops and other spheres are pushed around it, getting infected.
