1.2 - Design Criteria
The Design Criteria:
  • Should have a sleek design
  • Should have a modern, rounded design
  • Must easily hold on the user’s finger(s)
  • Should be comfortable
  • Has to be durable
  • Should be inexpensive to mass produce
  • Should be available in multiple colours
  • Shouldn’t easily slip from the user’s hands
  • Must Protect the phone’s front and back glass
  • Tab should be inline with the case
