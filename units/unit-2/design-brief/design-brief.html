2.1 - Design Brief
1. What is the specific situation exists that causes the problem requiring a solution to be made?

The vast majority of people don’t know or understand the serious consequences of something as simple as catching the coronavirus. Many go out of their way to protest against wearing a face mask, social distancing.

This is not intended to scare people away from socializing with others and going outside, it is intended to bring to light what can happen when someone catches the virus.

2. What situation exists that causes the problem requiring a solution to be made?
Everyone who carries a ph needs the solution, it is a benefit regardless of the situation they’re in.
3. Why is it important to solve this problem?
To raise awareness about the repercussions of catching the virus, and to educate people about foreseeability.
